About Me

Hello! I'm Vinay Sandula, a passionate Power BI Developer with expertise in transforming complex data into actionable business insights. My journey in data visualization began with a fascination for how visual representation can unlock the hidden stories within numbers.

With over 3 years of experience in the business intelligence domain, I've worked with organizations across various industries, helping them leverage their data assets to drive strategic decisions. My approach combines analytical thinking with creative design principles to create dashboards that are both powerful and intuitive.

I thrive in collaborative environments where I can translate business requirements into technical solutions, and I'm constantly exploring new data visualization techniques and technologies to expand my expertise.
